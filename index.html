About Us

More Than Just Golf

The Mosselkrakers are a close-knit group of senior golfers who gather at Mossel Bay Golf Club every Tuesday and Friday.

Competition is serious enough to keep everyone focused, yet friendly enough to ensure that laughter and camaraderie remain the true winners of the day.

Stableford competitions, Fourballs, Pairs matches, monthly Worsbraais and lasting friendships have become the foundation of the club.
