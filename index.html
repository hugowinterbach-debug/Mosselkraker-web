About the Mosselkrakers

The Mosselkrakers are a group of enthusiastic senior golfers who enjoy the challenge and camaraderie of regular golf at Mossel Bay Golf Club.

Members compete in Stableford events, Fourball matches and Pair competitions while enjoying one of South Africa's most scenic golf courses.

“See the sea from every tee.”
